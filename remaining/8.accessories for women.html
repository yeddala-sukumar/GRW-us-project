how to choose accessories

Step-01 : Consider the Occasion

Think about the occasion and the outfit you'll be wearing. The accessories should complement the overall look and feel appropriate for the event.

Step-02 : Start with a Focal Point

Choose one main accessory as the focal point of your outfit. This could be a statement necklace, a bold scarf, a pair of statement earrings, or a standout handbag.

Step-03 : Choose Accessories to Complement Your Features

Select accessories that enhance your natural features and body shape. For example, choose earrings that flatter your face shape or a belt that accentuates your waist.

Step-04 : Consider Color Theory

Use color theory to choose accessories that complement or contrast with your outfit. You can choose accessories in colors that match or harmonize with your clothing, or opt for complementary or contrasting colors for a bold statement.

Step-05 : Try On the Accessories with Your Outfit

Once you've chosen your accessories, try them on with your outfit to see how they look together. Make any adjustments or substitutions if needed to achieve the desired look.

optional accessories steps for men:

  • Coordinate Metals:

    If you wear jewelry, consider coordinating the metal tones to create a cohesive look. For example, if you're wearing gold earrings, opt for a gold necklace or bracelet to match.

  • Layer Accessories:

    Experiment with layering different accessories to add depth and interest to your outfit. For example, layering necklaces or stacking bracelets can create a stylish and eclectic look.

  • Consider a Scarf:

    In colder weather, a scarf can be both practical and stylish. Choose a scarf in a color or pattern that complements your coat or jacket. A wool or cashmere scarf can add warmth and texture to your ensemble.
