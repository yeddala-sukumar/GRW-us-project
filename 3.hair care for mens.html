Hair care

Step-01 : Shampoo

Wash your hair with a suitable shampoo based on your hair type (oily, dry, normal) and scalp condition. Massage the shampoo into your scalp using your fingertips, then rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step-02 : Conditioner

Depending on your hair's texture and condition, you may choose to use a conditioner. Apply conditioner primarily to the ends of your hair, avoiding the scalp, and leave it on for a couple of minutes before rinsing thoroughly.

Step-03 : Towel Dry

Gently pat your hair with a clean towel to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing vigorously to prevent damage and breakage.

Step-04 : Styling Products

If desired, apply styling products such as hair gel, pomade, wax, or mousse to achieve your desired hairstyle. Use a small amount and evenly distribute it through your hair, focusing on the roots or areas where you need hold or texture.

Step-05 :Hair Styling:

Use a comb, brush, or your fingers to style your hair according to your preference. You can blow-dry your hair for added volume or use a diffuser attachment for a more natural look.

Weekly or Bi-Weekly Hair Care:

  • Trimming:

    Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Visit a barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim, or if you prefer to trim your hair at home, invest in a quality pair of hair scissors and follow a tutorial for guidance.

  • Hair Mask:

    Use a hydrating or nourishing hair mask once a week to provide extra moisture and repair damage. Apply the mask to clean, damp hair, leave it on for the recommended time, then rinse thoroughly.

  • Hair Oiling :

    Once or twice a week, perform a hair oiling treatment to nourish and moisturize your hair and scalp. Choose a natural oil such as coconut oil, argan oil, or jojoba oil. Warm the oil slightly and apply it evenly to your scalp and hair, massaging gently with your fingertips. Leave the oil on for at least 30 minutes or overnight for deeper conditioning, then shampoo and condition as usual to remove the oil.
