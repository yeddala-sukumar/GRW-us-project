smokey make-up

Step-01 : Prep the Skin

Start with a clean, moisturized face. Apply a primer suitable for your skin type to create a smooth base for makeup application and to help makeup last longer.

Step-02 : Apply Primer

If desired, apply a mineral-based primer to create a smooth base for your makeup and help it last longer. This step is particularly beneficial if you have oily skin or large pores.

Step-03 : Foundation

Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone. Apply it evenly over your face and blend well using a makeup sponge, brush, or your fingers. Make sure to blend down your neck for a seamless finish.

Step-04 : Concealer

Use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover any blemishes or imperfections. Additionally, use a lighter concealer under your eyes to brighten and conceal dark circles. Blend the concealer seamlessly into your foundation.

Step-05 : Bronzer or Contour

Apply a bronzer or contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and around your hairline to add warmth and dimension to your face. Opt for a shimmery bronzer if you want to enhance the glow.

Step-06 : Blush

Choose a mineral blush in a shade that complements your skin tone. Swirl a blush brush into the powder, tap off excess, then smile and apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks. Blend upwards towards your temples for a youthful, flushed look.

Step-07 : Highlighter

Choose a shimmery highlighter in a shade that complements your skin tone. Apply it to the high points of your face, such as the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, the cupid's bow, and the brow bone. Blend well for a seamless, luminous glow.

Step-08: Eyeshadow

Select shimmery eyeshadows in colors that complement your eye color and outfit. Apply a light shimmery shade all over your lid as a base, then add depth with darker shades in the crease and outer corner. Highlight the inner corners of your eyes with a lighter shimmer shade for a brightening effect.

Step-09 : Eyeliner

Line your eyes with a pencil or liquid eyeliner to define them. You can create a subtle wing or keep it simple with a tight line along the upper lash line.

Step-10 : Mascara

Apply mascara to your upper and lower lashes to add volume, length, and definition. Consider using a volumizing or lengthening mascara for extra impact.

Step-11 : Eyebrows

Fill in your eyebrows using a matte eyebrow powder or pencil. Shape and define your brows to frame your eyes and complete your look.

Step-12 : Lips

Choose a matte lipstick or lip stain in a shade that complements your makeup look. Apply it evenly to your lips, starting from the center and blending outwards for a precise finish.

Step-13 : Setting Spray

Finish off your matte makeup look with a few spritzes of a matte setting spray. This will help lock in your makeup and control oil throughout the day.
