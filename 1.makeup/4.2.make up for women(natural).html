natural make-up

Step-01 : Start with Clean Skin

Begin by cleansing your face thoroughly to remove any dirt, oil, or impurities. Follow up with a lightweight moisturizer to hydrate your skin and create a smooth canvas for makeup.

Step-02 : Conceal Imperfections

Use a lightweight, buildable concealer to cover any blemishes, redness, or dark circles. Dab a small amount of concealer onto the areas that need extra coverage and blend it gently with your fingertips or a makeup brush.

Step-03 : Even Out Skin Tone with Foundation or Tinted Moisturizer

For a natural look, opt for a lightweight foundation or tinted moisturizer that matches your skin tone. Apply it sparingly and blend it evenly using a makeup sponge or brush, focusing on areas that need coverage while allowing your natural skin to show through.

Step-04 : Shape and Define Brows

Use a brow pencil, powder, or gel to shape and fill in your eyebrows for a polished look. Follow the natural shape of your brows and use short, feathery strokes for a soft and natural appearance.

Step-05 : Enhance Eyes with Neutral Eyeshadows

Choose neutral eyeshadow shades that complement your skin tone, such as soft browns, taupes, or peachy tones. Apply a light, matte shade all over the eyelid as a base, then add depth to the crease with a slightly darker shade. Blend well for a seamless transition between colors.

Step-06 : Curl Lashes and Apply Mascara

Curl your lashes with an eyelash curler to open up your eyes. Then, apply one or two coats of mascara to the top lashes, wiggling the wand from the roots to the tips for length and volume. Skip mascara on the bottom lashes for a more natural appearance.

Step-07 : Add a Hint of Blush

Choose a natural-looking blush color, such as soft pinks or peaches, to add a healthy flush to your cheeks. Smile and apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending it upward towards your temples for a subtle lift.

Step-08 : Highlight High Points of the Face

Apply a subtle highlighter to the high points of your face, such as the tops of your cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and cupid's bow. Opt for a natural-looking highlighter with a luminous finish to add a touch of radiance to your skin.

Step-09 : Finish with Lip Color

Choose a lip color that enhances your natural lip tone, such as a nude or MLBB (my lips but better) shade. Apply a lip balm for hydration, then add a sheer lipstick, tinted lip balm, or lip gloss for a polished yet effortless finish.

Step-10 : Set Your Makeup

Finish your natural makeup look by setting it with a lightweight setting spray to help your makeup last longer and give your skin a fresh, dewy finish.

Step-11 : Final Check

Take a final look in the mirror to ensure that your natural makeup look is subtle, fresh, and enhances your features while still looking effortless and radiant.
