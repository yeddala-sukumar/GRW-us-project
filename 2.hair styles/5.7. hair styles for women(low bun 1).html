LOW BUN-1

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be frizzy or flyaway, you can apply a small amount of hair serum or styling cream to smooth it out.

Step-02 : Brush your Hair

Use a hairbrush or comb to gently brush your hair and remove any knots or tangles. Brush your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step-03 : Secure with Hair Tie

Use a hairbrush or comb to gently brush your hair and remove any knots or tangles. Brush your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step-04 : Twist the Ponytail

Take the ponytail and twist it around the base to create a bun shape. Keep twisting until you've twisted all the way to the ends of your hair.

Step-05 : Wrap Around the Base

Once you've twisted the ponytail into a bun shape, wrap it around the base of the ponytail to form the bun. Tuck the ends underneath the bun and secure them with bobby pins.

Step-06 : Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Insert the pins vertically into the bun and cross them over each other for added hold and stability. Make sure to conceal the pins within the bun for a neat finish.

Step-07 : Smooth Out the Bun

Gently smooth out any bumps or imperfections in the bun with your hands. Use a comb or your fingers to tuck in any stray hairs and ensure the bun looks neat and polished.

Step-08 : Enhance Shine (Optional)

If you want to add extra shine to your low bun hairstyle, you can mist your hair with a light hairspray or shine-enhancing spray. This will give your bun a glossy finish and help hold it in place throughout the day.

Step-09 : Final Touches

Take a moment to check the overall shape and symmetry of your low bun hairstyle. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure the bun looks balanced and well-secured.

Step-10 : Final Look

Once you're satisfied with your low bun hairstyle, admire your elegant and sophisticated look in the mirror and confidently showcase your chic style!
