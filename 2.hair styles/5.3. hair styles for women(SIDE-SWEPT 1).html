SIDE-SWEPT 1

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. You can wash and condition your hair as usual before styling. If you have naturally straight hair and want to add some texture, you can use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. Alternatively, if you have curly hair and want a sleeker look, you can use a straightener to smooth out your hair.

Step-02 : Part your Hair

Create a deep side part in your hair using a comb or your fingers. You can choose which side you prefer to sweep your hair towards.

Step-03 : Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of hair serum or styling cream to your hair to add shine and control frizz. Focus on the lengths and ends of your hair, avoiding the roots to prevent greasiness.

Step-04 : Sweep your Hair

Sweep the majority of your hair to one side, starting from the deep side part you created earlier. You can use your fingers or a brush to gently guide your hair towards the desired side.

Step-05 : Secure with Bobby Pins

Once your hair is swept to the side, use bobby pins to secure it in place. You can crisscross the pins for added hold and stability. Place the pins discreetly underneath the top layer of hair to conceal them.

Step-06 : Create Volume (Optional)

If you want to add volume to the crown of your head, you can gently tease the hair at the roots using a fine-tooth comb. This will help create lift and dimension in your side-swept hairstyle.

Step-07 : Style the Ends (Optional)

Depending on your preference, you can leave the ends of your hair straight for a sleek look, or you can curl them for added bounce and texture. Use a curling iron or straightener to style the ends of your hair as desired.

Step-08 : Final Touches

Once you're happy with the placement of your side-swept hairstyle, give it a light mist of hairspray to set the style in place and add extra hold. Avoid over-spraying to prevent stiffness or buildup.

Step-09 : Accessorize (Optional)

add a touch of glamour to your side-swept hairstyle, you can accessorize with hair clips, decorative pins, or a stylish headband. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personal style.

Step-10 : Final Look

Take a moment to admire your side-swept hairstyle in the mirror and make any final adjustments if needed. Enjoy your elegant and sophisticated look!
