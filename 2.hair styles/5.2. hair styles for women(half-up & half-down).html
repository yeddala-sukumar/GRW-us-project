GRW-us Women's Section

half-up & half-down

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. You can wash and condition your hair as usual before styling. If desired, apply a small amount of styling mousse or hair serum to add texture and control frizz.

Step-02 : Part your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to create a clean part in your hair. You can part it in the middle for a symmetrical look or create a side part for a more asymmetrical style.

Step-03 : Gather the Top Section

Take a section of hair from the crown area (top of your head) and separate it from the rest of your hair. This section will be used for the "half-up" portion of the hairstyle.

Step-04 : Secure the Half-Up Section

Gather the top section of hair and gently pull it back towards the crown of your head. Use a hair tie or hairpins to secure this section in place. You can create a small ponytail or twist the hair and secure it with pins for a more elegant look.

Step-05 : Create Volume (Optional)

To add volume to the top section of hair, gently tease it with a comb or backcomb brush. This will help create a fuller, more voluminous look.

Step-06 : Style the Bottom Section

Let the remaining hair hang loose or style it as desired. You can leave it straight, add curls, or create waves depending on your preference.

Step-07 : Final Touches

Once you're satisfied with the placement of the half-up section and the styling of the bottom section, you can finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold everything in place.

Step-08 : Adjustments (Optional)

Take a moment to check the balance and symmetry of the hairstyle. You can adjust the positioning of the half-up section or smooth out any stray hairs for a polished finish.

Step-09 : Accessorize (Optional)

To add a touch of flair to your Half-Up Half-Down hairstyle, you can accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or hair ribbons. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personal style.

Step-10 : Final Look

Once you're happy with the overall look, admire your Half-Up Half-Down hairstyle in the mirror and confidently rock your new 'do!
