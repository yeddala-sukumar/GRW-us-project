LOW BUN-2

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. If you want to add texture and grip to your hair, you can apply a bit of texturizing spray or styling cream.

Step-02 : Brush and Gather Hair

Use a hairbrush or comb to smooth your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic or hair tie.

Step-03 : Loosen the Ponytail

Instead of pulling the hair all the way through the hair tie, leave a loop of hair sticking out on the last twist. This will create a loose looped bun.

Step-04 : Divide and Twist

Divide the looped section of hair into two equal parts. Twist each section around the base of the ponytail in opposite directions to create a twisted bun.

Step-05 : Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure the twisted bun in place. Insert the pins vertically into the bun and cross them over each other for added hold and stability. Tuck in any loose ends.

Step-06 : Messy Effect (Optional)

If you prefer a more relaxed and messy look, gently tug on the sides of the bun to loosen it and create more volume. This will give the bun a soft, tousled appearance.

Step-07 : Add Accessories (Optional)

To enhance the low bun hairstyle, you can add a decorative hair accessory such as a jeweled hairpin, flower clip, or ribbon. Secure the accessory to the side or center of the bun for an elegant touch.

Step-08 : Final Touches

Use a bit of hair serum or styling cream to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to the hairstyle. Gently brush over the surface of the bun to ensure it looks polished and neat.

Step-09 : Final Look

Once you're satisfied with your low bun hairstyle, take a moment to admire your chic and sophisticated look in the mirror. Adjust any stray hairs or accessories as needed for a perfect finish.
