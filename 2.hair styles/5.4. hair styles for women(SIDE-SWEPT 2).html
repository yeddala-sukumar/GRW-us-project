SIDE-SWEPT 2

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be flat, you can apply a bit of texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and grip.

Step-02 : Deep Side Part

Create a deep side part on one side of your head. You can use the arch of your eyebrow as a guide for where to start the part.

Step-03 : Sweep and Twist

Take the larger section of hair (the side with more hair due to the part) and sweep it over your head towards the opposite side. Gather the swept hair at the nape of your neck, slightly off to the side. Twist this section loosely towards the back of your head.

Step-04 : Secure with Pins

Use bobby pins to secure the twisted section in place at the nape of your neck. Cross the pins in an "X" shape to ensure a secure hold.

Step-05 : Create Waves (Optional)

If you want to add some texture and movement to the remaining hair, you can use a curling wand to create loose waves or curls. Alternatively, you can use a straightener to create sleek, straight strands.

Step-06 : Tuck and Pin (Optional)

Take the smaller section of hair (the side with less hair due to the part) and tuck it behind your ear. Secure it with a bobby pin to keep it in place and create a polished look.

Step-07 : Define and Finish

Use a bit of texturizing spray or hairspray to define the waves or curls and keep them in place. You can also gently tousle the hair with your fingers for a more effortless look.

Step-08 : Optional Accessories

Consider adding hair accessories like a decorative clip, headband, or ribbon to accentuate the side-swept style and add a touch of elegance.

Step-09 : Final Touches

Take a moment to check the overall balance and symmetry of your side-swept hairstyle. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure everything looks polished and secure.

Step-10 : Final Look

Once you're satisfied with the style, admire your side-swept hairstyle in the mirror and confidently showcase your chic and sophisticated look!
