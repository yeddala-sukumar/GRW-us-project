HIGH PONYTAIL-2

Step-01 : Preparation

Begin with clean, dry hair. If you have straight hair and want extra volume, you can apply texturizing spray or hair serum to add texture and grip.

Step-02 : Brush your Hair

Use a hairbrush or comb to smooth your hair, then gather it into a high ponytail position. Hold your ponytail firmly with one hand.

Step-03 : Secure with Hair Tie

Once your hair is gathered, secure it with a hair elastic or hair tie. Make sure the ponytail is positioned where you want it and that it feels secure.

Step-04 : Create Volume (Optional)

For added volume, gently tease the hair at the crown using a comb or backcombing brush. This step will create a more voluminous look at the top of your head.

Step-05 : Wrap Hair Around Elastic (Optional)

Take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the base to conceal the hair tie. Secure the end of the wrapped hair with bobby pins underneath the ponytail.

Step-06 : Style the Ends (Optional)

Depending on your preference, you can leave the ends of your ponytail straight for a sleek look, or you can add curls or waves using a curling iron or straightener for added texture and flair.

Step-07 : Fluff and Adjust

Use your fingers to gently fluff up the ponytail and adjust its shape. You can pull out a few strands near the crown to create a more relaxed, effortless look.

Step-08 : Optional Accessories

Consider adding a decorative hair accessory like a bow, ribbon, or jeweled hairpin to accentuate the high ponytail and add a touch of glamour.

Step-09 : Finish with Hairspray

To set your high ponytail in place and tame any flyaways, lightly mist your hair with hairspray. Hold the hairspray bottle about 10-12 inches away from your head to ensure even distribution.

Step-10 : Final Look

Once you're satisfied with your high ponytail, take a moment to admire your stylish and chic hairstyle in the mirror. Adjust any stray hairs or accessories as needed for a polished finish.
