BRAID STYLE

Step-01 : Preparation

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be frizzy or flyaway, you can apply a small amount of hair serum or styling cream to smooth it out.

Step-02 : Brush your Hair

Use a hairbrush or comb to gently brush your hair and remove any knots or tangles. Ensure your hair is smooth and free from any tangles before beginning the braiding process.

Step-03 : Divide your Hair

Divide your hair into three equal sections. You can do this by using your fingers or a comb to create three distinct sections of hair: one on the left, one in the middle, and one on the right.

Step-04 : Start Braiding

Take the section of hair on the right and cross it over the middle section, so it becomes the new middle section. Then, take the section of hair on the left and cross it over the new middle section. It will become the new middle section. Repeat this process, alternating between crossing the right and left sections over the middle until you reach the end of your hair.

Step-05 : Secure with an Elastic

Once you've reached the end of your hair, secure the braid with a hair elastic or hair tie. Make sure the tie is tight enough to hold the braid in place without causing discomfort.

Step-06 : Pancake the Braid(Optional)

To add volume and texture to your braid, gently tug on the outer edges of each section of the braid. This technique, known as "pancaking," helps to loosen the braid and create a fuller appearance.

Step-07 : Secure the Ends (Optional)

If desired, you can secure the ends of the braid with bobby pins to prevent them from unraveling. Simply tuck the ends underneath the braid and secure them with pins.

Step-08 : Hairspray(Optional)

If you want to add extra hold to your braid and prevent any flyaways, you can lightly mist your hair with hairspray. Hold the hairspray bottle about 10-12 inches away from your head to ensure even coverage.

Step-09 : Final Look

Once you're satisfied with your braid, take a moment to admire your hairstyle in the mirror. You can leave the braid as is or incorporate it into other hairstyles, such as a bun or ponytail.
